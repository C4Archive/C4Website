title: Idea Jam
-
synopsis: We'll be idea jamming at @thingtank on <b>September 8th, 2011</b>
-
rollover: @minilogo will be the focus of a new Idea Jam at ThingTank in Toronto on <b>September 8th, 2011</b> from <b>5-7 pm</b>
-
content: We're thinking about the future direction of @minilogo, and with the Idea Jam coming up at ThingTank we'll be brainstorming the following questions: 

> 1) (How) Does computational design fit with industrial design & prototyping?

> 2) (How) Would you use code to generate objects & prototypes in your design practice?

> 3) What functionalities should a coding framework for prototyping have?

<i>The event is free to attend, so please drop in!</i>

The event is scheduled on <b>September 8th, 2011</b> from <b>5-7 pm</b>, we'll be presenting second after Alan Majer's idea for home automation.

The address is: <b>376 Bathurst, Toronto, Basement Suite</b>

Also, check out @criticalmaking and @kmdi
